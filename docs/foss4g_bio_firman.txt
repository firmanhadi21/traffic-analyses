Firman Hadi is a lecturer at the Department of Geodetic Engineering, Universitas Diponegoro, Semarang, Indonesia. He holds a Ph.D. in Remote Sensing and Landscape Ecology from Institut Teknologi Bandung (2011). His research focuses on geospatial analysis and free and open-source GIS, with current work on large-scale spatiotemporal urban analytics. He has served as a geospatial specialist for The World Bank, the Japan Aerospace Exploration Agency (JAXA), and the Indonesia Ministry of National Development Planning. He is the lead developer of traffic-congestion-pipeline, an open-source Python package on PyPI that provides a reproducible workflow for traffic congestion analysis using PySAL, OSMnx, and statsmodels, applied to over 264 million observations across three Indonesian cities.
